The Activity of Self-Reflection Writing

Project 1: Old Language - New Image

I think about my "journey of self-discovery" every day, and to be able to express what I've been going through using art feels cathartic. It took a while, but I was able to find passages that described different stages of this "journey." The images themselves came naturally to me.

Project 2: Quietest Place

Even though my location was on campus, I had never been there specifically, and it was great. I got a chance to take a break from everything. With more resources, I would’ve gotten some better equipment to block out the wind, but I am very satisfied with the final result.

Project 3: Intimate, Indispensable GIF

Dungeons and Dragons helped me maintain my mental health during a period of extreme isolation. Because of that, D&D, and the people I play with, have become very dear to me, and I’m glad I was able to express that. Though very imperfect, I am satisfied with how the project turned out.

Project 3: Word as Image: Kinetic Typography

This project turned out to be a much bigger undertaking than I had anticipated. My ambitious idea and my lack of animation experience did not mix well. However, after many hours of work, the end result turned out to be what I was envisioning, so I think it was worth it.
