Artist Statements

Project 1: Old Language - New Image

This project, titled Past, Present, and Future, depicts three images that describe a personal journey of self-discovery. When I wanted the viewer to still be able to read the surrounding text, I used lighter colors, such as pink and blue. However, I used more black when that was less important. Having not many materials at my disposal, I made due with pens of various colors, and cut and rearranged the page. The rearranging of pages was inspired by a classmate, Victoria Messinger. The last image was partially inspired by M.C. Escher and his use of negative space.

Project 2: Quietest Place

My project, titled Respite, is a short video of the location I chose as my “quietest place.” The goal of this project was to sit in a relaxing environment and create music inspired by the surroundings. My aim was to create a work that embodied a time of rest in between the hustle and bustle of life. I used a portable USB piano, Ableton Live, and Adobe Premier. Once I found a spot, I got an idea for the music I wanted to make, then filmed and performed the work. Afterward, I edited it all together. The music was inspired by Levi Niha and FKJ.
